# Hutabyte 100x Growth Plan

Status: DRAFT — strategic vision, subject to quarterly re-evaluation
Horizon: 36 months (2026-05 → 2029-05)
Generated by /plan-ceo-review on 2026-05-28
Mode: SCOPE EXPANSION

This plan covers the path from 0 paying clients (Phase 0, today) to 100 paying clients across SME and mid-enterprise segments (Phase 4, month 36). It supersedes any prior informal growth assumptions in design.md.

## Strategic Premises Locked

1. **Growth model:** Platform + channel hybrid (Approach C). Build self-service product foundations from day 1, use channel partners to bootstrap year 1–2 demand, flip to self-service scaling in year 2–3.
2. **Segment sequencing:** SME-first (current design), expand up-market to mid-enterprise at Phase 3. Do not skip the SME validation phase.
3. **100x definition:** 100 paying clients across 3 segments (SME standard, SME shared, mid-enterprise) by month 36. Rp 2.5B–3.4B ARR depending on segment mix.

## Vision (36-Month Horizon)

Hutabyte is the default operations layer for Indonesian businesses adopting open-source business software. SME owner googles "Odoo Indonesia managed" → Hutabyte first answer. Mid-enterprise CFO evaluates ERPNext vs SAP → Hutabyte shortlisted. Bank embeds Hutabyte onboarding in SME corporate banking portal. Product is not the software — product is a stack that just works, in Bahasa Indonesia, with a contact who answers in 15 minutes.

Platonic ideal: An Indonesian mid-market CFO picks "Manufacturing ERP" from a catalog, signs digital contract, gets credentials by morning email, finance team uses Odoo by lunch.

## Cross-Phase Strategic Bets

1. **Indonesian-language brand-as-moat.** Phase 1 onward: every doc, support message, error string, contract is Bahasa Indonesia first. Localization is Phase 0 work, not Phase 4.
2. **age-encrypted client config as platform foundation.** Build admin UI, audit log, rotation automation. Use age multi-recipient (any-of-N) for Phase 0–3 — NOT M-of-N quorum, which age doesn't support natively and would require rolling Shamir secret sharing (eng-review E4: boring-by-default violation). Revisit HashiCorp Vault at Phase 4 if true quorum becomes a real customer ask.
3. **AI cost arbitrage.** Claude API at Rp 5K–10K/client/month is trivial vs Rp 200M+ revenue. Spend aggressively on AI-augmented support, deployment, incident classification.
4. **Compliance as mid-market wedge.** Phase 3: publish Hutabyte compliance whitepaper (UU PDP 2022, OJK, KOMINFO). Mid-enterprises buy compliance reassurance.
5. **Open-source contribution.** From Phase 2: contribute Indonesian localization to Odoo + ERPNext (Bahasa translations, IDR, e-Faktur PPN). Recruiting hook + credibility moat.
